  • Starter Package: ₦10,000 one-time onboarding fee activates your NTU (Network Traffic Unit) earning account.

  • Call Earnings (NTU): Every call you make generates Network Traffic Units — converted to naira daily. Earn while using your phone normally.

  • Data Usage Bonus: Your internet data usage earns passive income. The more you browse, the more you earn.
